# CoreBank - Customer User Guide

## Welcome to CoreBank!

This guide helps you understand your bank accounts and how to perform common operations.

---

## 1. What You Need to Know

### Your Customer ID
- Format: **CUST*** (e.g., CUST001)
- Used to identify you in the system
- Provided when you open an account

### Your Account Numbers
- Format: **ACC*** (e.g., ACC1001)
- Each account is independent
- You can have multiple accounts
- Found on your account opening confirmation

### Account Types Available

**Savings Account**
- Perfect for: Personal savings
- Interest Rate: 4% per year
- Minimum Balance: ₹1,000
- Best for: Earning interest on savings

**Current Account**
- Perfect for: Business operations
- Interest Rate: 0%
- Minimum Balance: ₹5,000
- Best for: Unlimited transactions, no interest requirement

**Fixed Deposit (FD)**
- Perfect for: Long-term investment
- Interest Rate: 7.5% per year
- Tenure: 6 months to 5 years
- Best for: Safe, guaranteed returns

## 2. Opening Your Account

**What You Need**:
- Personal identification (PAN, Aadhar)
- Contact information (Phone, Email, Address)
- Initial deposit amount

**Process**:
1. Visit our branch with required documents
2. Fill out account opening form
3. Choose account type
4. Make initial deposit (minimum required)
5. Receive your Account Number

**Minimum Deposits**:
- Savings: ₹1,000
- Current: ₹5,000
- FD: Any amount

## 3. Basic Operations

### Check Your Balance

**How to Check**:
1. Visit any branch
2. Tell teller your Account Number
3. Teller displays your current balance

**What You See**:
- Current Balance
- Account Type
- Account Status
- Last Transaction

### Deposit Money

**In-Person Deposit**:
1. Visit branch during working hours
2. Bring cash and your Account Number
3. Provide amount to deposit
4. Receive deposit confirmation

**What Happens**:
- Your balance increases immediately
- You receive a receipt
- Transaction appears in your statement

### Withdraw Money

**In-Person Withdrawal**:
1. Visit branch with withdrawal form
2. Provide Account Number
3. Specify withdrawal amount
4. Verify identity
5. Receive cash and receipt

**Important Rules**:
- You must maintain minimum balance:
  - Savings: ₹1,000
  - Current: ₹5,000
- Daily withdrawal limit: ₹50,000
- Contact us if you need more info

**Example**:
```
Account Balance: ₹25,000
Minimum Required: ₹1,000
Can Withdraw Maximum: ₹24,000
```

### Transfer Money to Another Account

**How to Transfer**:
1. Tell teller you want to transfer
2. Provide receiving account number
3. Specify amount to transfer
4. Confirm transaction
5. Receive transfer reference number

**What You Get**:
- Confirmation of transfer
- Reference number for tracking
- Updated account balance

## 4. Understanding Interest & Returns

### Savings Account Interest

**How It Works**:
- Bank pays you interest on your balance
- Interest rate: 4% per year
- Calculated monthly, credited to account
- You keep earning interest on interest (compound interest)

**Example**:
```
Your Balance: ₹10,000
Annual Interest Rate: 4%
Interest Earned in One Month: ₹33.33
After 1 Year: ₹10,408.33
```

### Fixed Deposit Interest

**How It Works**:
- You lock your money for a fixed period
- Higher interest rate: 7.5% per year
- Cannot withdraw before maturity
- Interest paid at end of tenure

**Example**:
```
FD Amount: ₹1,00,000
Tenure: 1 Year
Interest Rate: 7.5%
Interest Earned: ₹7,500
Maturity Amount: ₹1,07,500
```

### FD Premature Withdrawal

**If You Need Money Early**:
- You can withdraw before maturity
- Interest reduced by 2%
- Less common to do this
- Contact us for details

## 5. Loans Available

### Types of Loans

**Personal Loan**
- Purpose: Any personal need
- Amount: Up to ₹10,00,000
- Interest: 12% per year
- Tenure: Up to 5 years
- EMI Example: ₹2,00,000 loan = ₹6,644/month for 3 years

**Home Loan**
- Purpose: Buy/build a house
- Amount: Up to ₹50,00,000
- Interest: 8.5% per year
- Tenure: Up to 20 years

**Car Loan**
- Purpose: Buy a vehicle
- Amount: Up to ₹15,00,000
- Interest: 9% per year
- Tenure: Up to 7 years

**Education Loan**
- Purpose: Fund education
- Amount: Up to ₹20,00,000
- Interest: 7% per year
- Tenure: Up to 15 years

**Business Loan**
- Purpose: Business operations
- Amount: Up to ₹1,00,00,000
- Interest: 10% per year
- Tenure: Up to 10 years

### Applying for a Loan

**What You Need**:
- Valid customer account
- Proof of income
- Identification documents
- Purpose of loan

**Process**:
1. Visit branch and discuss loan options
2. Complete loan application form
3. Provide supporting documents
4. Wait for approval (typically 3-5 days)
5. If approved, amount is transferred to your account
6. Start paying monthly EMI

### EMI (Monthly Payment) Explained

**What is EMI?**
- Fixed monthly payment
- Includes principal and interest
- Same amount every month
- Automatic debit from your account

**Example EMI Calculation**:
```
Loan Amount: ₹2,00,000
Interest Rate: 12% per year
Tenure: 36 months (3 years)

Calculated EMI: ₹6,644 per month
Total Amount Paid: ₹2,39,184
Total Interest: ₹39,184
```

### EMI Payment

**How Payment Works**:
- Automatic debit on fixed date each month
- Amount comes from your linked account
- Payment is recorded in statement
- If account balance is low, payment fails

**What If I Miss Payment?**
- Contact us immediately
- Additional interest may apply
- May affect loan approval for future
- Discuss payment arrangement with manager

### Prepayment (Pay Loan Early)

**Benefits of Prepayment**:
- Save on interest
- Close loan faster
- Improve credit score

**Process**:
- Contact us anytime
- Pay remaining loan amount
- We recalculate remaining EMIs
- Loan is closed

## 7. Security Tips

### Protect Your Account

**Password Security**:
- Never share your password
- Change password regularly
- Use strong password (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)

**Account Number Safety**:
- Don't give account number to strangers
- Verify recipient before transfer
- Keep account statements secure

**Your Responsibilities**:
- Inform us if card is lost
- Report suspicious transactions
- Keep contact information updated

## 8. Common Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How often is interest credited?**
A: Interest is credited monthly to your account.

**Q: Can I change account type?**
A: No, but you can open a new account of different type.

**Q: What if I forget my account number?**
A: Contact our customer service with ID proof. We'll provide it immediately.

**Q: How do I increase withdrawal limit?**
A: Visit branch with valid ID. We can adjust limits based on your profile.

**Q: Is there a fee to open account?**
A: No, account opening is free. No maintenance charges.

**Q: What happens to interest if I close FD early?**
A: Interest is reduced by 2%. Please contact us for exact calculation.

**Q: How do I pay loan EMI?**
A: EMI is automatically debited from your linked account on due date.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes! You can have Savings, Current, and FD accounts simultaneously.
